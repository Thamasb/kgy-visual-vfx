What is the VFX service? Visual Effects in its most basic definition, refers to a digital image being created or manipulated by a VFX artist. This could be creating something that can't be done in the real world, or altering live-action footage to enhance it.
What does VFX include?
Visual effects (VFX) are any special effects added to a film using a computer. VFX typically combines live-action footage and manipulated or enhanced imagery to create realistic environments and characters. The main goal of VFX is to take something fake and make it look real, or at least believable.
